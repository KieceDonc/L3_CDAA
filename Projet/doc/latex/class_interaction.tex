\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
An interaction is a block of text tagged with @\+TODO and @\+DATE to indicate tasks and sets the date of said todos. 

Class for an \mbox{\hyperlink{class_interaction}{Interaction}} specifications. An instance of \mbox{\hyperlink{class_interaction}{Interaction}} will always contain a string (content) and its date of creation (date) through two attributes. The content of an interaction will always be a single string -\/ And the syntax of an \mbox{\hyperlink{class_interaction}{Interaction}} is as follow \+:~\newline
~\newline
 {\itshape \mbox{[}\mbox{\hyperlink{class_interaction}{Interaction}} comment\mbox{]} \mbox{[}{\bfseries{@\+TODO}} todo n°1 \mbox{[}{\bfseries{@\+DATE}} dd/mm/yyyy\mbox{]}\mbox{]} \mbox{[}{\bfseries{@\+TODO}} todo n°2 \mbox{[}{\bfseries{@\+DATE}} dd/mm/yyyy\mbox{]}\mbox{]} . . . . . \mbox{[}{\bfseries{@\+TODO}} todo n°k \mbox{[}{\bfseries{@\+DATE}} dd/mm/yyyy\mbox{]}\mbox{]}}~\newline
~\newline
 With bracketed elements being optional~\newline
 And k between 0 and infinity (please don\textquotesingle{}t do this) /!\textbackslash{} We do NOT treat the content line by line.
